Doherty Career

The New Mexico native was initially trained by her mother at Fishback Studio of the Dance and continued her classical training at the San Francisco Ballet School. She became a trainee before being given the opportunity to develop a new musical under the direction of Susan Stroman.

At the age of 15, Juliet was a featured TEDx speaker. She spoke about empowering young artists and people alike to achieve their dreams through mindful practice. Juliet performs as an international guest artist, and most recently appeared in the role of “Lise” in the Tony award-winning musical, An American in Paris, at the Hale Centre Theatre

Juliet Doherty Awards and Achievements

She is a two-time gold medalist at the largest dance competition in the world, the Youth America Grand Prix.

Juliet has also taken home prizes from the National Society of Arts & Letters, and the Beijing International Ballet Competition.
