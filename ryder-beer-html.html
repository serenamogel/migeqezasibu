Ryder Beer - Age, Family, Bio

Younger brother of singing sensation Madison Beer. He and Madison have shown how close they are over social media. He is also a music producer and has attended the Berklee College of Music in Boston. 

Before Fame

He and Madison were raised in Jericho, Long Island, New York.

Trivia

He reached over 160,000 followers on Instagram. In December of 2021, he posted a photo with his sister at the Spider-Man: No Way Home premiere and tagged the Dolce&Gabbana fashion company in the caption. 

Family Life

His parents are Robert and Tracie Beer. They divorced and his father remarried.

Associated With

His sister has received support from Justin Bieber, who has shared more than one of her YouTube links.
