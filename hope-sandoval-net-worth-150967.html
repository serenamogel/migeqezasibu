Hope Sandoval Net Worth

Hope Sandoval is an American singer and songwriter born on June 24, 1966 in Los Angeles, CA. She is best known for her work with the alternative rock band Mazzy Star and her solo project Hope Sandoval & the Warm Inventions. She has released several albums and singles throughout her career, and is considered one of the most influential female artists of the 1990s.
