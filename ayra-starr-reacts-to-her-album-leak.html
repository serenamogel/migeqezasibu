Ayra Starr Reacts To Her Album Leak

Young sensational Nigerian songstress and entertainer, Oyinkansola Sarah Aderibigbe, professionally known as Ayra Starr has reacted to the leak of the impending deluxe version of her debut album, ’19 & Dangerous’.

The Mavin Records star girl and the Gen Z Afrobeats ambassador has taken to her official social media pages to react to the leak of her yet to be released deluxe version of her debut album, ’19 & Dangerous’.

ALSO: Wizkid Shares Teaser For ‘Bad To Me’ Music Video – WATCH

Ayra Starr’s ’19 & Dangerous Deluxe Version’ leaked 12 days to the slated release date (Friday, October 14, 2022). Reacting in a now deleted post, the ‘Bloody Samaritan’ hit-maker said; “My album got leaked 😔”

Currently, ’19 & Dangerous Deluxe Version’ which has 6 new records still has the four unreleased tracks; Track 3, Track 5, Track 9 and Track 14 on display as an untitled record.

MEANWHILE: Renowned Nigerian music video director and cinematographer, TG Omori a.k.a Boy Director has acknowledged the role Burna Boy played in his rise to fame.

The ace music video director and 2022 Headies Awards winner for Best Music Video Director has taken to social media to reveal how the highly rated Nigerian Afrobeat recording and performing artiste has inspired him.

According to him, Burna Boy inspires him to reach for greater heights and to continue to top himself. In a tweet, TG Omori wrote; “Burna boy is so so inspiring can’t even lie. Brother literally inspired me to standout from being an everyday Director to possessing so much star power unknowingly.”

This comes after the successful shoot and release of Asake’s ‘Joha’ which surpassed 2 million views in two days. TG Omori and Burna Boy teamed up to bring the visuals of his ‘Odogwu’ single to life. He has also worked with Olamide, Wizkid, Tekno, Kizz Daniel, Fireboy DML, Falz, Timaya, Naira Marley, and many others.
