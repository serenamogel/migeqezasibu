Amber Marchese Bio (Wiki)

Who is Amber Marchese?

Amber Marchese is an American TV star and entrepreneur.

She rose to fame after appearing on a reality show for The Real Housewives of New Jersey in 2014.

She was also seen in The Real (2013) and Marriage Boot Camp: Reality Stars (2016).

Amber Marchese- Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

She was born on 13 Aug 1977 in New Jersey, the United States. The details of her father and her mother are unknown.

Amber belongs to Caucasian ancestry. Regarding academics, she was a student of BBA at Monmouth University.

After passing out from there, Amber Marchese joined Teachers College, Columbia University, and studied bio-behavioral studies and exercise physiology.
