Quentin Tremaine Jammer Wiki Biography

Quentin Tremaine Jammer (born June 19, 1979) is currently a Free Agent and an American football cornerback who last played for the Denver Broncos of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football for the University of Texas, and earned All-American honors. He was drafted by the San Diego Chargers in the first round of the 2002 NFL Draft and he played for them for eleven seasons. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
