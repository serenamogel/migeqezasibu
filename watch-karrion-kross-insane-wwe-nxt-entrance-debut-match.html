WATCH Karrion Kross Insane WWE NXT Entrance &amp; Debut Match

Karrion Kross and Scarlett are finally in WWE NXT. They made quite an entrance to the black and gold brand as well.

Click here for our complete coverage of WWE NXT from this week.

Karrion Kross used to be “Killer Kross” in Impact Wrestling, but WWE had to alter his name. What they took away from his name they more than made up for in presentation. He had an amazing entrance alongside his real-life girlfriend Scarlett Bordeaux, now just known as Scarlett.

Kross won his enhancement match very quickly. It was over before the smoke settled.

It was quite an impressive entrance into the WWE Universe. Expect great things from him.
