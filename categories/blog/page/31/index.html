How do you treat bougainvillea leaf spots? Remove badly infected leaves. Collect and dispose of fallen leaves. It is generally not necessary to put bougainvillea plants on a regular spray schedule with fungicides for this fairly common problem. Why does my bougainvillea have white spots?
