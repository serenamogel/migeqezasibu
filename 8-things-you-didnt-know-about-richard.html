8 Things You Didnt Know About Richard Dillane

➡ Richard Dillane Biography

Richard Dillane is a British actor, who has worked in many films and has earned a highest place in the film industry by giving stellar performances during his career. Also, he is popularly known for his role as OSS agent Peter Nicholls in Ben Affleck’s 2012 political thriller titled “Argo”. Furthermore, he has built his career as a film and television actor, radio artist as well. There are more things to know that his fans want to know about him. And we are presenting some unknown things you didn’t know about.

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Richard Dillane

Richard Dillane’s birth & family background

He was born in 1964, England, Uk where he raised and grew up with his elder brother, Stephen Dillane under the supervision of parents (John Dillane & Bridget Dillane). In 2005, he got married, Jayne McKenna, and the couple is blessed with a son, named, Murray Dillane.

He has a degree in Philosophy

He has done his graduation from the University of Manchester, Manchester, England from where he earned a degree in Philosophy.

His associations in the film industry

Richard Dillane has associations in the film industry as the most acclaimed actors Frank Dillane & Seamus Dillane are his nephews.

His career on British television

He was raised and grew up in England but he moved to Australia where he worked as a white-water rafting guide. However, later, started working on British television in the mid-90s.

He also acted in stage productions

Richard Dillane also made his name as a theatre artist and acted in stage productions at the National Theatre in London & The Royal Shakespeare Company as well.

He also narrated an audiobook

Besides acting, he is a good narrator and has narrated an audiobook titled “Stranger House” written by Reginald Hill.

He & social media

Richard Dillane has a huge fan following on all his social media accounts where he shares updates, photos, and videos to enthrall them.

He is a multi-talented star

He is a multi-skilled star and is known for his extraordinary capabilities. Besides acting, the British actor has worked as a radio artist.
