Jessica Lange Net Worth

Jessica Lange is an acclaimed actress who has won numerous awards for her performances. She was born in Cloquet, Minnesota on April 20, 1949 and won the Best Actress Academy Award in 1983 for her role in Tootsie. She has also won a Screen Actors Guild Award for her role in Grey Gardens and has been praised for her roles on the FX series American Horror Story and Feud: Bette and Joan.

About

Won the Best Actress Academy Award in 1983 for her role in Tootsie and appeared in Prozac Nation. In 2009, she won a Screen Actors Guild Award for her role in Grey Gardens.
