Jeremy Allen White Children: Meet Ezer Billie &amp; Dolores Wild White

Jeremy Allen White children-American actor, Jeremy Allen White was born on February 17, 1991, in Brooklyn, New York City in the United States of America.

Who are Jeremy Allen White’s children?

Jeremy Allen White and his estranged wife, Addison Timlin have two daughters together; Ezer Billie White who was born in October 2018, and Dolores Wild White who was born in December 2020.

Jeremy Allen White’s career

In 2006, White landed his first acting roles in the television series Conviction and the movie Beautiful Ohio. He was cast in the pivotal role of Phillip “Lip” Gallagher in the Showtime series Shameless in 2011. In 2018, he also starred in Homecoming.

White was chosen to play the title character in the Hulu series The Bear in 2022. In the role, he portrays Carmen “Carmy” Berzatto, a disturbed but talented chef from New York who returns to his birthplace of Chicago in an attempt to turn around his failing restaurant.

He and co-star Ayo Edebiri attended training at the Institute of Culinary Education to be ready for the part.

He received a Golden Globe for Best Actor in a Television Series Musical or Comedy, a Critics’ Choice Television Award for Best Actor in a Comedy Series, and a Screen Actors Guild Award for Outstanding Performance by a Male Actor in a Comedy Series for the role.

For the 2023 Primetime Emmy Award, he was a nominee for Outstanding Lead Actor in a Comedy Series.
