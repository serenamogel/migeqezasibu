Sydney Sweeney College: What did Sydney Sweeney study?

Sydney Bernice Sweeney is an American actress. She shot to fame through the Netflix series Everything Sucks!. She played Emaline and has portrayed Eden in the Hulu series The Handmaid’s Tale.

Sweeney also features as Alice in the HBO miniseries Sharp Objects in 2018.

Since 2019, she has starred as Cassie Howard in the HBO teen drama series Euphoria and as Olivia Mossbacher in The White Lotus. In the film, she had a role in Quentin Tarantino’s 2019 comedy-drama Once Upon a Time in Hollywood, portraying Snake, a member of the Manson family.

Sydney was born on September 12, 1997, in Spokane, Washington. Her mother is a lawyer and her father works in the medical field.

She has a brother, Trent. Sweeney was raised in northwestern Idaho in the state’s Panhandle region along the Washington border, at a rural lakeside home.

Sweeney attended Saint George’s School in Spokane. She is a trained MMA fighter and competed in grappling in high school.

Sydney Sweeney College: What did Sydney Sweeney study?

Sydney Sweeney went to college in Los Angeles and studied Entrepreneurship at Lehman Alternative Community School ( LACS).
