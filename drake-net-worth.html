The Toronto Mansion

In 2016, Drake bought 87,120 square feet of land in Toronto worth $6.7 million where he dreamt of building a home for himself.

This custom-made palace-like mansion located in the Bridle Path of Toronto is now over a $100 million estate.

Being a Canadian, Drake holds a great hometown pride, and this home is his primary residence.

Designed by the famous Canadian planner Ferris Rafauli, the house is also featured in Drake’s song Toosie Slide.

The house boasts lavish features such as NBA full-size OVO basketball court, indoor pool, sauna, gym, AI bathroom, huge chandeliers, and whatnot.

It has been reported that the master suite alone is twice big a size as that of a regular apartment, including two dressing rooms and a steam room.

Further, there are four more bedrooms in the mansion.

Hidden Hills Mansion

Drake calls this California house a “YOLO Estate,” which he purchased in 2012 for $7.7 million.

Originally, the house was built in 1974, which the artist later renovated, taking four years. The mansion spread on 12,500 square feet, features six bedrooms and ten bathrooms.

This lavish pad boasts every kind of luxury one can dream of, such as a gym, library, spa room, 25-seat theater, warm tub, wine cellar, game room, and many more.

Furthermore, the outside of the house is gorgeous as well. The exterior reason sports a tennis court, basketball court, and even a volleyball court situated above the main mansion.

Likewise, there are stall houses, several bathrooms, viewing pavilions along with an over-the-top swimming pool, waterfalls, rock waterslide.

The third home in Hidden Hills

    Career

    Early Life

    Aubrey Drake Graham was born in October 1986 in Toronto, Canada. Since his childhood, he grew up with music as his father Dennis was a drummer and his mother Sandi also dated back to a musical family. Similarly, Drake’s uncle was a bassist.

    The Grammy-winning singer has a mixed ethnicity due to his parent’s origin, and he addresses himself as Black and Jewish. He was only five years old when his parents divorced, after which his mother raised him in a Jewish community.

    Drake is a school dropout which he did for pursuing acting. He started from a teen soap named Degrassi: The Next Generation, where he played a wheelchair-bound persona by the name of Jimmy Brooks for seven years.

    Leaving the show, the singer signed a label named Young Money Entertainment owned by Lil Wayne, after which his career mounted. Eventually, Drake became one of the biggest rappers universally.
