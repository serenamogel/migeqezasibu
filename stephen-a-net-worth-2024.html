Early Life and Career Beginnings

Stephen A. Smith was born in the Bronx, New York, and raised in the Hollis section of Queens. He played basketball in high school and received a scholarship to attend Winston-Salem State University, a historically black university in North Carolina. Smith’s career in journalism began with a print media focus, writing for various newspapers before transitioning into radio and television.

Smith’s dynamic personality and expertise in sports analysis led him to television, where he became a fixture on ESPN. He initially appeared as a commentator on various shows before becoming a host on “First Take,” where his debates with Skip Bayless became must-watch television for sports fans.
