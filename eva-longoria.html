Eva Longoria

In 2018, Eva Longoria’s name appears among the hundreds of women who have been blessed with a child or who are expecting a kid. She also revealed her baby belly through her Twitter account with a step into the new year. Let’s learn more about the wiki of the stunning American actor and activist!

Eva Longoria’s real name is Eva Jacqueline Longoria Baston, and she was born on March 15, 1975. She is one of the most talented actresses, producers, directors, and businesswomen in the United States. Corpus Christi, Texas is her hometown. She is 42 years old and will turn 43 in March of this year. Eva got her start in the entertainment sector by playing minor roles in various TV shows. However, her work on the ‘Desperate Housewives’ project catapulted her to fame. She is one of the actors who has been featured in magazines such as People and businesses such as L’Oreal and Hanes.

Salary and Net Worth

Eva Longoria net worth is not in the millions because she has worked in this profession for a long time. Her per-episode compensation for the show ‘Desperate Housewives’ was $400,000. And an annual remuneration estimated to be close to $7 million is recorded.

Eva’s genuine net worth is $35 million as of 2017. She enjoys shopping and vacationing. Her net worth is expected to increase by $3-$5 million .

Married Life, Husband, and Divorce

Eva has been married three times. Tyler Christopher was the first man she married in her life. They developed close because they were both in the same profession and married in 2002. However, they divorced in 2004 after only two years of marriage.

Following her breakup with Tyler, she began dating NBA player Tony Parker. In 2004, the two began hanging out together. After two years of dating, they decided to take the next step and were engaged on November 30, 2006. The devoted pair married at Paris City Hall on July 6, 2007. This marriage lasted one year longer than the previous one. Both cited “irreconcilable disagreements” as the reason for their divorce in November of 2010. They divorced on the 28th of January, 2011.She had been out of the dating pool for over four years after two failed marriages. She and her boyfriend, Jose Baston, exchanged engagement rings on December 13, 2015. On that day, the majority of her fans and following learned of her connection. The lovely pair married on May 21, 2016, following a five-month courtship.

Children, Baby Bump

The news of Eva’s pregnancy was revealed for the first time on December 19, 2017. People Magazine reported she’s four months pregnant and that she’ll give birth in June 2018.

Eva is about to give birth to her first kid. On New Year’s Day 2018, she posted a photograph of her developing baby bulge on her Twitter and Instagram accounts. On her social media pages, she has received a deluge of congrats and well wishes.
