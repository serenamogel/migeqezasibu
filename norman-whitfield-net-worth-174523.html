Norman Whitfield Net Worth

Norman Whitfield was a renowned songwriter and producer born on May 12, 1940 in New York City, NY. He is best known for his work with Motown Records, where he wrote and produced classics such as "War" and "I Heard It Through the Grapevine". His work was instrumental in creating the Motown sound that has become iconic in the music industry.
