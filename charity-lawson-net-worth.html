Charity Lawson Early Life and Education

Charity Lawson was born on December 30, 1995, in Columbus, Georgia. She graduated from Columbus High School in 2014. Later, she attended Auburn University and earned her Bachelor of Science in Rehabilitation and Disability in 2018.

Following that, Charity completed her Master of Education in Clinical Mental Health Counseling from Auburn in May 2022. She joyfully announced her achievement of a Master’s Degree in a post on Instagram. She wrote,

“I will be graduating with my Masters in Clinical Mental Health Counseling, from Auburn University. The joy that comes with obtaining a degree the second time around is so much sweeter when you gain confirmation that it is exactly one of the things God has called you to do.”

What is Charity Lawson Professional Background?

While she was studying, Charity worked at Mint Julep Boutique Company from 2015 to 2016 as an Inventory Lead Associate. From 2018 to 2019, she was a camp counselor at Full Life Ahead Company and also worked as a special education counselor in the Muscogee County School District.

Right now, Charity is an intern therapist at the Juvenile Advocacy Center in the Tri-County CAC. In 2023, she had the opportunity to appear on TV for the first time in a dating reality show, The Bachelor Season 27, which aired on ABC.

Charity Lawson has a stunning smile and looks great. She is 5 feet 7 inches tall (170 cm) and weighs about 65 kg (143 lbs). Lawson has dark skin, black hair, and lovely dark eyes.
