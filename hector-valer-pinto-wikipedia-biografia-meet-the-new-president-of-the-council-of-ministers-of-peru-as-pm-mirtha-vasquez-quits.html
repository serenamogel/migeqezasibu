Hector Valer Pinto is the new president of the Council of Minister leading Peru, as the former president Mirtha Vasquez quits her position. Learn more about the president, his Wikipedia biography, age, wife, and net worth.

Valer Pinto is a lawyer and politician from Apurimac. He kickstarted his political career as a candidate for the Regional Presidency of Lima but was not elected. Then, in the 2021 general elections, he was elected congressman of the Republic for Popular Renovation and finally made it to the president of the council of ministers.

Héctor Valer Pinto took over as the third president of Pedro Castillo’s Council of Ministers on Tuesday, February 1, replacing Mirtha Vásquez. Vasquez had been in office for about five months but resigned after Avelino Guillén was removed from the portfolio of the Ministry of the Interior.

President Hector Valer Pinto Wikipedia Biografia: Biography Explored

Hector Valer Pinto is a parliamentarian for the period 2021-2026, currently based in Lima, Peru.

He completed his primary and secondary education at Colegio No 665 – Cachora and the Gran Unidad Escolar Miguel Grau. From 1982 to 1992, he studied Law and Political Science at the Inca Garcilaso de la Vega University, as well as from 1995 to 1998 at the Universidad Libre in Colombia.

He also studied Rural Development at the Pontificia Universidad Javeriana de Colombia and Criminal Law at the Universidad Mayor de San Marcos for his master’s degree. Since 2012, he has been the General Manager of Valer Abogados SAC Legal Center.

Hector Valer Pinto Edad: What Is His Age?

As of 2022, Hector Valer Pinto is 63 years of age.

Valer Pinto was born on February 4, 1959, in the Department of Apurimac. He was elected as the president of the council of ministers three days prior to his 63rd birthday.

By the age of 30, Valer Pinto had a double degree in Law and Political Science in his hand. Ever since he completed his master’s degree and had been working under several law firms alongside entering politics in his late forties.

Hector Valer Pinto Esposa: Wife And Family Explored

Hector Valer Pinto was once married to his lovely wife but he is now widowed.

His wife passed away on October 6th, 2021. However, the reason behind her death is unknown to the public. With some heavy emotional words, the president said goodbye to his wife last year.

There is not much news about his wife and family in the internet database. Being in one of the highest positions in the country, he has kept his private life as private as possible.

This may have to do with the security and safeguarding of their family members, as politicians and their family members are prone to several dangers and threats.

Hector Valer Pinto Net Worth

Hector Valer Pinto is estimated to have a net worth of more than $1 million.

Being a lawyer attorney for more than two decades and working as the General Manager of one of the top legal firms in the country, he is easily worth multi-million dollars.
